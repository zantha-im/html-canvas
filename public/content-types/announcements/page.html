Saturday, October 4, 2025

Warehouse relocation

Important information on excise stock

We are relocating our warehouse. During the move, our excise duty bond is temporarily suspended, which affects stock that requires the bond.

We have a two-week window to clear bonded items. Remaining stock must be incinerated under customs supervision.

What this means for you

These products are priced at cost (plus required duty for UK). Restocking pauses until the new bond is in place.

Thursday, September 25, 2025

Backlog cleared: We’re back on track!

US shipping resumed; operations normalized

Following changes to the US de minimis rule, shipping paused on Aug 22. We resumed on Sep 16 and have since dispatched 2,008 parcels with a single-day high of 339 orders.

A note on shipping costs

Why costs are high right now — and what’s next

  • Seven-Day Express is our current option: We reconfigured service to conform to new rules; more affordable rates are in progress.
  • Your tariff is pre-paid: We currently absorb tariff costs to avoid surprise fees and speed customs clearance.

A look ahead: We’re working on more affordable options and exploring a dedicated US warehouse.

Navigating the storm

How we overcame the US shipping crisis & got back to business

A global shipping shutdown

On Aug 29, 2025, changes to the US de minimis rule made all low-value parcels subject to tariffs, disrupting global logistics.

Timeline highlights

  • Aug 22: We absorb a new Royal Mail tariff to protect customers.
  • Aug 29: Global carriers suspend US shipping.
  • Sep 9: We integrate a compliant courier and resume shipping.
  • Sep 12: Brief pause as the new process is tested.
  • Today: US shipping fully restored; backlog clearing.

Our commitment

  • We pay all tariffs & duties: No surprise fees on delivery.
  • Fast, compliant shipping: Express service with pre-paid duties.
  • Transparent updates: We'll keep you informed as things evolve.
